About

A foodie herself, Maria hails from Kanpur and swears by the city’s flavourful and appetising street food.
Her blog aptly titled as Flavour of Kanpur shares street food recipes, regional recipes, spicy Goan cuisine as well as eggless baking recipes.
Maria’s blog also has a separate ‘Budget’ and ‘Low-fat’ section where you can get recipes that can be prepared quickly with minimum expenditure and to shed off those extra kilos, respectively.
